Community Kitchen Event: Nourishing Our Neighbors

Join us for a heartwarming Community Kitchen Event, in partnership with Einstein Bros. Bagels, as we come together to prepare and serve nourishing meals to our neighbors in need. This event is an opportunity for us to make a real difference in the lives of those facing hunger and food insecurity in our community. Whether you're a seasoned volunteer or looking to lend a hand for the first time, everyone is welcome to join us in spreading warmth and kindness. Together, we can make a meaningful impact, one meal at a time.

  222 Main St. Dallas, TX, 75201

  May 15, 2024

  10 a.m. - 2 p.m.


Note: All necessary safety protocols and guidelines will be followed to ensure the well-being of our volunteers and participants. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.
