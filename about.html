Our Story

At Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands, we are driven by a deep sense of compassion and a commitment to serving the unhoused, unsheltered, and the less fortunate in our community. Our organization is founded on the belief that ministry and service begin in the heart, and we are dedicated to sharing the message of Jesus Christ through our actions, one full belly at a time.

We seek to reach out to those who suffer spiritually, emotionally, and physically. We currently have ongoing feeding programs in Kenya Africa, and have adopted a special needs home there to support with food. We have donated two wheelchairs to help with mobility-impaired children. Our domestic cities of service include Dallas, Fort Worth, Grand Prairie, and surrounding areas, and Texarkana.

Since its origin, Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands has prepared, distributed, and or sponsored almost 100,000 meals. We partner with various non-profit organizations.
